Monthly Sales Overview – March 2025

March saw a strong increase in revenue across multiple categories, with a total sales growth of 18.4% compared to February. The electronics and fashion departments were the top performers, contributing over 60% of overall earnings.

Conversion rates improved by 2.5%, thanks to the newly implemented checkout experience and updated product recommendation engine.

The marketing campaigns focused on social media and email newsletters saw a CTR of 7.3%, above the industry average.

For the coming month, we plan to roll out automated cart recovery emails and begin beta testing our AI-powered pricing tool.
